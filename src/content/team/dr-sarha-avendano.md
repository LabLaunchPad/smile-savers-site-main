Dr. Sarha Avendaño is a compassionate dentist dedicated to providing individualized, high-quality care to patients across Queens. After earning her B.A. in Psychology and Biochemistry from New York University, she completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) at the NYU College of Dentistry in 2019.

## Clinical Background & Experience

Following dental school, Dr. Avendaño completed a rigorous General Practice Residency at New York Presbyterian - Queens in 2020. She then spent three years serving the community on The Bad River Reservation in Northern Wisconsin before returning home to Queens to continue her practice.

## Personalized Philosophy of Care

"My favorite part about dentistry is being able to tailor-make a treatment plan for each person who comes to see me," says Dr. Avendaño. "Every individual is unique and deserves an approach that fits their specific needs and goals. There is nothing more gratifying than knowing you've made a positive difference in someone's life."
